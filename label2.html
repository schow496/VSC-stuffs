Mushstool (2015)

Phil Ross


From his Yamanaka Series, Phil Ross utilizes fungi for his completely biodegradable furniture. Based in San Francisco, sustainability is central to Ross' identity and work. Ross uses a fungi called Reishi that is sprouting up in alternative medicine, used as an anti anxiety treatment - his stools very well may be a health tonic. Along with medicinal purposes, Ross' shroomy stools are lightweight, stronger than concrete, fireproof, waterproof, and mold resistant. Should one grow tired of their stool, they can simply bury it and it will decompose entirely.
