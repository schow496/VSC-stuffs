Muskin


Muskin is a 100% fungi-alternative to animal leather. Made from a large parasitic fungus (Phellinus ellipsoideus), this alternative grows in the wild on the trees of the subtropical forests. From a tactile approach, Muskin can be similar to suede with a soft, velvety-like touch. As the it ages, Muskin, can become a durable, cork-like material that can be good for highly functional objects, such as shoes.
