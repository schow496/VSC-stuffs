Immersive Experience

Join us in a completely immersive experience and discover how psychedelic mushrooms affect you. Be warned: this experience includes strange sounds, strobe lighting, and funhouse-like attractions that may not be enjoyable for everyone. This exhibition is limited to visitors 18+.



Living Collection

Explore our living collection featuring different types of mushrooms in our Funky Forest. Learn about the four categories of mushroom species: saprotrophic, mycorrhizal, parasitic, and endophytic and discover your favorite species out of the 10,000 we have to provide. Stick around for our daily interactive workshops to learn which mushrooms you can cook with and which ones you should avoid.



Musical Mushrooms

Our Musical Mushrooms room is dedicated to the inner workings of the mushroom. This exhibition is designed to teach visitors how the cellular activity of mushrooms produces a bioelectrical voltage that rushes through the wires and is translated into angelic and alien sounds. This immersive experience is open to all ages.
