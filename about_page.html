Our Mission

The Museum of Mushrooms is a non-profit 501(c)(3) dedicated to educating people about the role that fungi play in our natural world from past, present, and future. The Museum of Mushrooms strives to engage and connect its visitors with the dynamic world of fungi through innovative exhibitions and educational programs.


Our Vision

The Museum of Mushrooms (MOM) will be a vibrant cultural and social landmark in the city of Portland, Oregon. It will serve as a both a tourist destination and educational attraction for locals and visitors alike.
