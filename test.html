The Museum of Mushroom

Our Mission

The Museum of Mushrooms is a non-profit 501(c)(3) dedicated to educating people about the role that fungi play in our natural world from past, present, and future. The Museum of Mushroomsstrives to engage and connect its visitors with the dynamic world of fungi through innovative exhibitions and educational programs.
